Ellie Evans
Obsessed With Me

For this one-shot music video, I collaborated with artist Ellie Evans and Director Jeevan Dominguez to bring their vision for Obsessed with Me to life. Captured on an ARRI Alexa 35 paired with Ultra-prime lenses, the project relied on soft, warm lighting—created using a China ball soft-box—to craft an intimate yet emotionally charged atmosphere. The single continuous take follows Ellie, seated at the head of a family dinner table, singing with a distant, introspective presence. Her performance reflects a deep sense of disconnect between her and her family.
