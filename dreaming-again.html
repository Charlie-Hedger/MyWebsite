Ellie Evans
Dreaming Again

For Dreaming Again, I worked with Director Jeevan Dominguez and Artist Ellie Evans and to create a visually immersive journey through the cycles of a single day, all set within the intimate confines of her bedroom. Using a ceiling-mounted Sony FX6 paired with a vintage Yashica 15mm fisheye lens, we captured an expansive and dreamlike perspective that added a unique, character-rich quality to the visuals.

Three distinct lighting setups marked the progression of time—morning, golden hour, and night—crafted with an Aputure 300D and a spotlight attachment. A Venetian blind gobo cast textured, dynamic shadows, enhancing the mood and transforming the space.
