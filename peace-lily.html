Peace Lily

Peace Lily is a short timelapse film exploring the dynamism of a common houseplant. Captured using a RED V-Raptor VV and brought to life with a programmed move on the Kessler CineShooter, the film reveals the graceful rise of a Peace Lily as it recovers after watering.

Shot over several hours, the piece uses slow, deliberate motion control to elevate a simple act of plant behaviour into something cinematic and emotive. The camera sweeps gently around the subject, creating a sense of scale and drama that mirrors the plant's transformation.
