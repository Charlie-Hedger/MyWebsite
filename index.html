ABOUT

Charlie is a Camera Technician with five years of expertise in the rental and camera service industry. With a strong technical background in filmmaking technology he has honed a deep understanding of camera equipment and technical processes, making him a trusted support figure for a wide range of high-profile clients.

Beginning his career as a Trainee Tech in the ARRI Service Department, Charlie quickly developed a comprehensive skill set in camera technology, progressing to Camera Tech roles at multiple Rental Houses. Over the years, he has provided technical support for major productions, including BBC Natural History Unit’s Blue Planet, BBC Drama’s Father Brown, and corporate clients such as Vice.

Charlie is actively involved in independent film projects, working across various camera department roles. Whether serving as a Focus Puller, Camera Operator, or Director of Photography (DOP), he brings a strong creative vision and technical proficiency to short films and music videos.

Charlie is passionate about staying at the cutting edge of camera technology and filmmaking techniques, constantly refining his craft to offer top-tier service and creative collaboration.
